Research and development

I am a member of the research group of Professor Christoph Kirsch. My second project in collaboration with Google Munich deals with the question if we can optimize the performance by taking care of locality in memory management.

ACDC4JS, University of Salzburg and Google Munich (2013-2014)

Research and development

In collaboration with Google Munich we work on benchmarks for JavaScript virtual machines, especially Google's V8. The benchmarks will show how memory management and garbage collection may be improved. My main tasks are working on heap models, creating automatic user interaction and modifications of virtual machines to obtain data for analysis.
